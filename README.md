# 90-Day Health Tracker

A 90-day health checklist app for tracking daily steps, water intake, sleep, and workout minutes.

## Features

- Generate a 90-day plan from a start date
- Track daily progress for:
  - 10,000 steps
  - 3 liters of water
  - 8 hours of sleep
  - 30–60 minutes of workout
- Save daily values and automatically mark days complete
- View progress and completed day count

## Use

1. Open `health-90-day-tracker/index.html` in your browser.
2. Choose a start date and generate the 90-day plan.
3. Select a day from the list and fill in your daily values.
4. Save the day to track completion.

## Notes

- The app stores your plan in browser `localStorage`.
- You can reopen the tracker later and continue where you left off.
